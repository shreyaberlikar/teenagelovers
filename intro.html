When It All Began

Aavni was a Second Year Computer Engineering student, studying in the buzzing, fast-paced city of Pune. Darshan, on the other hand, was pursuing Second Year Mechanical Engineering in their quieter hometown of Latur. Despite the distance, a thread connected them — something deeper than geography.

Both of them were born and raised in Latur. Their story didn’t start in college. It began when they were just five years old — two tiny souls seated side-by-side on the same school bench. The friendship was pure, effortless, and playful.

They were inseparable — until life took its turn.

After 7th grade, things shifted. Aavni changed schools. Darshan stayed back. Daily conversations faded into silence. They never fought. They just… stopped.

No goodbye. No closure. Just distance and growing silence — like life had hit pause.

But destiny — subtle and strange — wasn’t done yet.

After 10th, a message turned into a smile. A memory turned into a laugh. Slowly, gently, they began talking again — this time, with new understanding and deeper hearts.

What began in a classroom as childhood friendship... was now blossoming into something far more beautiful.

And this... is their story. Welcome aboard.
