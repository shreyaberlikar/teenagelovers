Chapter 1: Wings in the Air!

Date: 09/10/2024

"May your coffee kick in before reality does!" …read the playful quote scribbled on the side of the coffee mug, handed to Aavni by the McDonald's waiter. She couldn't help but smile — the kind of lighthearted humor that perfectly fit this moment.

Aavni glanced across the table at Darshan, taking in his effortless charm — a pistagreen shirt that made his eyes shine, a sharp nose, perfect eyebrows, and those irresistible lips curled into a mischievous smile. Her heart fluttered.

Dressed in her favourite yellow shirt and jeans, her hair pulled back in a half-ponytail, she felt a little more vibrant sitting across from him.

A waiter approached. Aavni exchanged a look with Darshan — they both realized they were supposed to order downstairs.

"I'll be back, right?" Darshan chuckled nervously and headed off. A few minutes later, he returned, proud but slightly puzzled. Aavni smiled, savoring the moment.

The waiter returned again, this time with just coffee. Confused, they looked at each other — clueless.

"The combo set of coffee & burger," he said. Then added innocently, "Idk, mala nahi mahiti, jevdha dila tevdha khau, rahile tr rahu det!"

Aavni burst out laughing, her eyes crinkling at his adorable expression — a mix of pride and confusion. Their glances spoke a thousand words.

When the burger finally arrived, he pulled her chair gently. “Sit here,” he said, a playful smile forming.

She moved beside him. They giggled, shared bites, clicked selfies, and filmed little videos. Each moment felt like a memory blooming in soft pink.

Between bites, stories, and laughter, she glanced again at the quote: "May your coffee kick in before reality does!" How fitting it felt — this day felt whimsical, unreal, perfect.

As they wrapped up, laughter still lingering, Aavni smiled and suggested their next plan — a nearby garden stroll. The date had just begun. But it already felt unforgettable.
