Dear Reader,

Thank you for making it this far.

For flipping through the emotions, the mess, the magic, and the madness of young love.

This isn’t just a story of Aavni and Darshan—
It’s for every teenager who fell too hard, cried silently, forgave loudly, and still believed in love.

I don’t know what love means to you, but I hope these pages felt real.

If it brought a smile, a tear, or even a memory—then this story has done its job.

Thank you for reading my first book.
Thank you for believing in a narrator who just wanted to write something raw and real.
And most importantly, thank you for feeling it.

Stay soft. Stay strong.
And never stop believing in your kind of love.

With all my heart,
Shreya
