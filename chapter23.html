Chapter 23:
The Girl Behind the Blocklist

"It wasn’t the betrayal that broke her. It was the fact that she stayed after every red flag, thinking love would be enough."

August 12, 2023. Aavni would never forget that date.

A few days before, Darshan had casually asked, "Hey, give me your Insta ID and password?"
She didn't even think twice.
"Why not?" she thought. "Trust is everything."
She typed out the credentials and sent them without hesitation. No questions. No doubts.

Two days later, she noticed something strange. A girl named Anjali had been blocked. She hadn't blocked anyone.
Curious, she asked him, "Darsh... who’s Anjali? Why is she blocked from my account?"
His reply came instantly:
Darshan: "She’s a relative... kind of. She stalks me a lot. I don’t want her knowing about us. She might tell my family things."

Something about the tone felt off. But Aavni didn’t press further. She simply replied:
Aavni: "Okay. I trust you."

But the thought lingered.
So, two days later, she quietly unblocked Anjali... and sent her a follow request.
Anjali accepted.

At 6:42 PM, her phone buzzed with a message request:

Anjali: “Hey. Are you Darshan’s girlfriend?”
Aavni: “Yes, and I always will be.”

A pause. Then came the message that made her breath catch.

Anjali: “I was his girlfriend too.”

Aavni’s hands trembled. She typed:
Aavni: “What do you mean?”
Anjali: “I was with him for the past few months. I didn’t know about you until recently.”
Aavni: “Since when?”
Anjali: “March. We met through mutual friends. We met often... we kissed... we’ve been close.”
Aavni: “Are you serious?”
Anjali: “Yes. He told me I was the only one.”

Then she sent a photo—cuts on her wrist.
Tears blurred Aavni’s vision. Her heart cracked in places she didn’t know existed.

Anjali: “I wish none of this happened. I really liked him.”

Aavni sat back, shattered. She had trusted him with every part of her soul. And now—
She blamed herself.
Maybe I was too emotional. Too possessive. Too much.
But she wasn’t.
She was just a girl in love—with a boy who didn’t deserve her.

Her fingers hovered over his chat.
Aavni:
“You gave the gift I gave you... to your friend.
You blocked a girl on my account, and lied about her being a relative.
And now I find out… she was your girlfriend too?
I trusted you. But you were never mine to trust.”
