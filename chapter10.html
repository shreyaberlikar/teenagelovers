Chapter 10: Secrets & Sass

Late Night Gossip 🌙

It was past 9 p.m., and Aavni had just finished cleaning up the cups and folding the blanket from the bed. Her room still smelled like him — warm chai and citrusy perfume.

She was barely back in her pajamas when her phone rang.

Incoming Call: Tani 💅

“ARE YOU ALIVE?” Tani screamed the moment Aavni picked up.

Aavni giggled, “I am… barely.”

“Omg bitch tell me everything! Did he show up with flowers? Did he kiss you before entering? Or did y’all jump right into—”

“Can you stop narrating like a Wattpad scene?” Aavni laughed. “He just came in. Sat on the sofa like a good boy.”

Tani gasped. “So no kiss? No hug? Just water and namaste?”

Aavni smirked. “Well… I did shut the door and lock the gate.”

“Oh-hooooo, shubh shubh bol na!”

They both burst into laughter.

Spilled Tea, Literally 🍵

“So what happened?” Tani asked, dropping her voice like a gossip aunty.

“Did he… y'know… take a sip of chai or sip from you first?”

Aavni grabbed her pillow and squealed into it.

“Taniii, stop!”

“Nope, I’m not stopping. You told me to teach you how to kiss using your fingers — upper lip, lower lip, breath in-out... don’t waste my tutorial now!”

Aavni laughed so hard she nearly dropped her phone.

“Okay fine,” she confessed. “I used it.”

Tani gasped like a proud mother. “Andddd?”

Aavni whispered, “He was shook.”

They both screamed in sync.

The Flashbacks 🫶

“I swear,” Aavni began, lying back on her bed, “When I opened the door, he looked nervous but so damn cute. And when I kissed him... he froze. Then pulled me so close I forgot how to breathe.”

Tani’s voice softened, “Girl… your first real kiss at your place… with nobody home. That’s some movie sh*t.”

“I know,” Aavni said, suddenly quiet. “It felt… perfect. Not rushed. Not awkward. Just us.”

Tani smiled through the phone. “That’s love, idiot. Told you na — one day he’ll kiss you and all your drama will melt.”

Aavni grinned. “And you’ll forever be the girl who taught me kissing with my fingers.”

“Put that in your wedding speech!” Tani howled.

🌙 The Last Message

Before hanging up, Tani asked, “So what’s next? When’s Round 2?”

Aavni giggled. “Maybe next week. But you’re dropping me off again if he comes.”

Tani winked through the phone, “As long as you promise to tell me every damn detail.”

“Promise. Upper lip, lower lip and all.”

“Behind every girl in love… is a bestie who taught her how to kiss with her own hand.”
