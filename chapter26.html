Chapter 26:
Now…

“Some love stories don’t restart. They continue—after a pause.”

With new chapters, new promises, and old hearts learning how to beat together again.
Aavni never believed in second chances. Not until Darshan gave her a reason to believe people could change—if the love was strong enough.

This time, he showed up.
Not just with words, but with actions.
No more disappearing. No more excuses.
He messaged first. He asked about her day. He remembered test schedules, favorite snacks, and the silly songs she liked to hum.

He made time—no matter how busy.
He visited Pune more often.
They met in the same garden near her PG, sat on their favorite bench with chai in hand.
Laughing. Talking. Healing.

Their McDonald's dates made a comeback too—
where he’d still mess up her order on purpose
just to hear her pretend-angry rants.

Darshan wasn’t perfect.
But he was trying. Every day.
And Aavni was slowly learning to trust again— not blindly, but with hope.

Because sometimes,
love doesn’t start over.
It just continues… differently. Gently. Stronger than before.
